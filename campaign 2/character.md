# Campaign 2 — Player Character

> The MC. Built against the campaign rules: classless (defined by background/training/traits/gear), logic-leaning resolution, HP/CR/DC, growth via **relics (burst)** + **Emperor's background essence (passive)**.

---

## Identity
- **Name:** **Kaelen "Kael" Voss**
- **Origin:** prodigy of **Halvern**, a medium-sized town in the current empire (living world).
- **Love interest:** **Liora Wynn** — also of Halvern (the person he was burned trying to save).
- **Status at story start:** mortally burned saving Liora from an escaped dragon; body **kept barely alive on life support** (dead-enough-but-recoverable). **Soul sent to the Veil.**
- **Progression:** **0%** (just arriving in the Veil).

## Concept
A mind, not a muscle. Kael wins by **reading a problem until its weak point is obvious** — the crack in the wall, the gap in a ward, the flaw in a creature's pattern — then applying the smallest precise effort there. Dangerous to underestimate, easy to physically overwhelm if you can force the fight to last.

## Personality *(confirmed)*
- **Analytical & observant** — narrates the world in terms of how it works and where it breaks.
- **Calm while thinking, anxious while enduring** — composed when there's a puzzle, rattled by raw attrition.
- **Pragmatic problem-solver** — prefers cleverness, leverage, and exploits over brute force.
- **Quietly protective** — the kind of person who runs *toward* the fire for someone he loves (already proven).
- **Arrogant — ignores warnings** — thinks he's better than everyone; dismisses caution and others' advice. He **will** push past the 50% settlement's warning.

## Attributes
Resolution: **d20 + attribute modifier vs DC**. Advantage/disadvantage for situational swings.

| Attribute | Mod | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Acuity** (perception, spotting flaws/exploits) | **+4** | Signature strength |
| **Logic** (systemic thinking, analysis, deduction) | **+4** | Signature strength |
| **Arcana** (arcane/psychic manipulation) | **+3** | Prodigy pool, but young & untrained for Veil intensity |
| **Resolve** (mental endurance, will) | **+2** | Matters for surviving the essence "flood" |
| **Agility** (reflexes, finesse) | **+1** | Average |
| **Endurance** (physical stamina, toughness) | **−1** | **FLAW** — tires fast, low HP |

## Vitals
- **HP:** **9** (low — driven by the Endurance flaw; he cannot trade blows for long).
- **Psychic Might:** an **oversized bucket** in the living world (prodigy for his age) → scales toward **drum-tier** in the Veil. Deep pool for a youth, but **no training for the Veil's pressure** and far below the era's strongest (water-tower) mages.

## Traits *(classless definition)*
- **Exploiter** — *advantage* when identifying a weakness/flaw/fault-line/exploit in an object, structure, creature, ward, or system.
- **Systems Mind** — *advantage* to understand mechanisms, patterns, plans, and how complex things interconnect.
- **Keen Senses** — *advantage* on perception to notice details, changes, or hidden things.
- **Arcane Aptitude** — manipulates arcane/psychic essence **flexibly** (no fixed spell list); effects adjudicated by **logic + cost**, scaled vs DC.
- **Frail** *(flaw)* — *disadvantage* on sustained physical exertion, prolonged combat, forced marches, and physical attrition; lowest HP tier; **must end fights fast or avoid them.**

## Starting Loadout
- **Non-magical gear:** whatever he had in the living world **does NOT transfer** to the Veil.
- **Magical artifacts:** **none yet** (relics are how he'll grow — burst power spikes).
- Effectively arrives in the Veil with **his mind, his arcane aptitude, and nothing else.**

## Growth Levers (reminder)
- **Relics** → instant power spikes (find / equip / bind).
- **Emperor's background essence** → constant passive growth (stronger nearer the palace, within survivable limits).
