# Campaign 2 — Quick Reference (TL;DR)

> One-page recap of everything built so far. Full detail: `world.md` + `character.md`.

## System
- **DnD-inspired, classless, logic-leaning.** Outcomes follow fiction/logic by default.
- **Dice only for genuine uncertainty with stakes** (or truly random events). No rolling for trivial/near-certain things.
- Keeps **HP, DC, CR**; checks = **d20 + modifier vs DC**; advantage/disadvantage for swings.
- Characters defined by **background/training/traits/gear**, not classes or spell lists.

## Cosmology
- Three dimensions: **Living World**, **The Veil**, **The Afterlife (Dead)**.
- Magic strength: Dead > Veil > Living. Restriction: Dead (most) ; Veil & Living (unrestricted).
- **Veil = best place to wield magic** (strong + unrestricted).

## The Veil (mechanics)
- **Enter** by being "dead enough" (soul leaves the living) **but body still recoverable**. Hard to do.
- **Exit:** body healed → return to Living; body destroyed → Afterlife (can't will this from inside; very rare exceptions).
- **Gear:** non-magical gear doesn't transfer; **bound magical artifacts do**. Artifacts are rare, not legendary-rare.
- **Inhabitants:** psychic creatures, golems, and friendly soul-remnants (= the ~50% peaceful settlement).

## History
- Ancient civ **moved into the Veil ~1,450y ago** (Great Transfer) via automated-labyrinth life-support; built a mega-empire that thrived a **millennium**.
- **The Fall (~450y ago):** simultaneous **Cat-5 tornado + mag-8 quake** broke life support → citizens damned to the afterlife → empire collapsed overnight.
- **The Emperor** resisted the universal order **50–60 years**; others burned out. Only the Emperor still matters.
- Living-world ruins look "primitive" only because they predate the Transfer; the empire is gone now anyway.
- Current civ: single empire, **~500 years = all of recorded history**. First ancient structure found ~300y ago ("mass grave").

## The Emperor & Palace (the goal)
- Corpse on a golden throne; wears the **5 most powerful relics** (equipable/bindable, never taken).
- Leaks an undirected **psychic-essence beacon** (÷8 per distance-doubling): beneficial far out, **drowning** near the throne.
- Throne room base: **3 glass-encased relics** (use-in-place, non-movable), incl. **The Sundering Needle** (cuts a temporary hole between dimensions → the way home).
- **100 steps** from base to throne; essence rises to a flood near the body. Deflector relics buy time, never safety → climb fast.

## Power Scale (psychic might)
bucket (normal mage, Living) → drum (Veil) → 5 drums (Dead) → water tower (strongest current mages) → **ocean** (live Emperor's peak) → **Caspian Sea** (Emperor's corpse now).

## MC Growth
- **Relics = instant power spikes.** **Emperor's background essence = constant passive growth.**

## Progression (player %)
0% start → 1–10% half-in-the-Veil → 10–50% grind (golems/spirits/relics) → **50% peaceful settlement warns to turn back** → 50–90% ruins/routine/emptiness → **90–95% HARD+ failing palace defenses** → 95–100% palace maze + throne room → **100% return to the living world.**
